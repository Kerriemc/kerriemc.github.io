About Me

Hi, I'm Kerrie McNamara, a Licensed Broker and Realtor® working with Jack Conway in Mattapoisett. I specialize in helping clients navigate their real estate needs throughout the SouthCoast area of Massachusetts.

I believe in the power of personalized service. Wherever you are in your real estate journey, my goal is to ensure you feel supported and informed every step of the way.

I'm a lifelong SouthCoast resident, living on beautiful West Island in Fairhaven with my family, I understand the unique charm of our local communities. My own real estate journey began with my own first home purchase at 22, and since then, I've gained extensive experience buying, selling, renovating, building, and managing properties.

I'm passionate about helping you navigate the real estate market with confidence, whether you're a first-time buyer, seasoned investor, or looking to sell.
